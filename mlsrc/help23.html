This program does addition and subtraction of two numbers in degree-minute-second (dd.mmss) or hour-minute-second format (hh.mmss). It can also multiply a degree/hour-minute-second-format number by an ordinary scalar.

First, enter a number n in dd.mmss or hh.mmss format and press  A .

Next, to do addition, enter the second number p in dd.mmss or hh.mmss format and press  B ; the display will contain the answer.

Or, to do subtraction, enter the second number p in dd.mmss or hh.mmss format and press +/- followed by  B ; the display will contain the answer.

Or, to multiply by a scalar, enter the value a and press  C ; the display will contain the answer.

Or, to divide by a scalar, enter the value a and press  D ; the display will contain the answer.

Register used: 01.
