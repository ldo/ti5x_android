ML-11 — Triangle Solution (1)

This program will work out various parameters of a triangle given some of them: given either all three sides (SSS), two sides and a non-included angle (SSA), or two sides and the included angle (SAS), it will compute the remaining angles or sides.

Knowing SSS

First, enter the sides of the triangle, in any order:

  • Enter side a: a  A 
  • Enter side b: b  B 
  • Enter side c: c  C 

Then, compute angle A: 2nd A´

Now, you can compute the remaining angles, in either order:

  • angle B: 2nd B´
  • angle C: 2nd C´

Knowing SSA

First, enter the known sides and the angle, in any order:

  • Enter side a: a  A 
  • Enter side b: b  B 
  • Enter angle A: A  C 

Then, compute side c:  D .

Now, you can compute the remaining angles, in either order:

  • angle B: 2nd B´
  • angle C: 2nd C´

Knowing SAS

First, enter the known sides and the angle, in any order:

  • Enter side a: a  A 
  • Enter side b: b  B 
  • Enter angle C: C  C 

Then, compute side c:  E .

Now, you can compute the remaining angles, in either order:

  • angle B: 2nd B´
  • angle A: 2nd C´

Formulas Used

A + B + C = 180° or 2π

c2 = a2 + b2 - 2ab cos C

a ÷ sin A = b ÷ sin B = c ÷ sin C

Registers used: 01-04.
