ML-25 — Unit Conversions (2)

This program does conversions between various volume, weight and temperature units of measurement. Note that it can convert to or from either Imperial or US volume units, based on the setting of flag 6.

First, select between Imperial or US units for volume conversions as appropriate (this setting does not affect conversions of other units):

  • Imperial units (default): INV 2nd St flg 6.
  • US units: 2nd St flg 6.

Next, enter the value in the units to convert from, and press the appropriate conversion key to display the value in the units to convert to, as follows:

  • Temperature, °F to °C:  A 
  • Temperature, °C to °F: 2nd A´
  • Volume, Imperial/US fluid ounces to litres:  B 
  • Volume, litres to Imperial/US fluid ounces: 2nd B´
  • Volume, Imperial/US gallons to litres:  C 
  • Volume, litres to Imperial/US gallons: 2nd C´
  • Weight, ounces to grams:  D 
  • Weight, grams to ounces: 2nd D´
  • Weight, pounds to kilograms:  E 
  • Weight, kilograms to pounds: 2nd E´

Flags used:

  • 6 — set to convert to/from US units, clear to convert to/from Imperial units.

Registers used: none.
