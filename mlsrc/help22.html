ML-22 — Checking/Savings Account Management

This program calculates transactions (deposits and withdrawals) on checking and savings accounts. It can also accumulate interest, but only between transactions.

First, enter opening balances:

  • Checking balance: C.Bal 2nd A´  E 
  • Savings balance: S.Bal 2nd B´  E 

Select which account to enter transactions for:

  • Checking account: 2nd A´ or
  • Savings account: 2nd B´

Then, enter transactions:

  • Deposit: Dep.amt.  B  or
  • Check/Withdrawal: Chk.amt.  C 

The new balance will be displayed after each transaction. Repeat the transaction-entry steps as necessary.

To calculate interest on the selected account:

  1. Enter annual interest percent (%I): %I/yr 2nd C´
  2. Enter compounding periods per year: P 2nd D´
  3. Enter total number of periods to accumulate interest for: N  D 

The new balance will be displayed after the last step. Step 3 can be repeated at a later point (e.g. after entering more transactions) without having to re-enter steps 1 and 2.

Registers used: 01-10.
