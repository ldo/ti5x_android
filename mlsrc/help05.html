ML-05 — Complex Functions

First, enter the complex number X which is the operand for the remaining operations: enter the real part of X, then press  A , then enter the imaginary part, and press  A  again.

The remaining functions are:

 B 
Convert to angular format: shows r, press x⇌t to show θ.
 C 
Compute X2: shows real part of result, press x⇌t to see imaginary part of result.
 D 
Compute square root of X: shows real part of result, press x⇌t to see imaginary part of result.
 E 
Compute 1/X: shows real part of result, press x⇌t to see imaginary part of result.
2nd A´
Compute natural log of X: shows real part of result, press x⇌t to see imaginary part of result.
2nd B´
Compute eX: shows real part of result, press x⇌t to see imaginary part of result.

Registers used: 01-04.
