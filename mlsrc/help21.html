The game is to guess a secret number (whole numbers only) form 1 to 1023 that has been generated in the calculator. The calculator responds with 1 for "too high", -1 for "too low", or 0 if "correct". Your score (number of guesses) is then displayed by the calculator.

You may also challenge the calculator to guess a number you've chosen.

You guess

  • Key in number (0 to 199017)  A 
  • Generate secret number  B 
  • Enter your guess (1 to 1023)  C 
    • Clue: -1. if guess was low
    • 1. if guess was high
    • flashing 0. if your guess was correct
  • Display score  D 

Calculator Guesses

  • Select a number (1 to 1023)
  • Display calculator's first guess 2nd  A' 
  • If calculator's guess is Low 2nd  B' 
  • If calculator's guess is High 2nd  C' 
  • If calculator's guess is Correct 2nd  D' 

Registers used: 01-05.
